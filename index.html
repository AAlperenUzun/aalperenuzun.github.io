About

Developer, designer,
and team lead.

I'm a game developer based in Bursa, Turkey with over 5 years of professional experience building games in Unity. From hyper-casual mobile titles to a fully custom 2D action roguelite, I've worked across the full spectrum of game development.

At Sharaido Games I lead a small team building The Dark Crown: Genesis — handling everything from enemy AI architecture and procedural generation to technical art, UI/UX, and game design. I believe great games come from developers who can think holistically.

Previously at MOBGE, I shipped 30+ hyper-casual prototypes and two titles that together reached over 30 million downloads on iOS and Android.

Featured Project

The Dark Crown: Genesis

A 2D action roguelite set in an endless cursed dungeon. Sacrificed by your kingdom, you fight back — defeating powerful Demons and claiming their elements.

Pixel-perfect Unity URP. Procedurally generated dungeons via Edgar Pro. Enemy AI on NodeCanvas behavior trees — each enemy has its own state machine, attack interrupts on damage, and unique mechanics. I also handled all VFX, normal maps, emission maps, UI systems, and game design.

Playable demo live on Steam now.
